# Health and Energy

## Goals from past direction

- Weight loss / leaner body
- Strength and athleticism
- Smoking cessation
- Better sleep
- Training consistency
- Simple Greek-cuisine nutrition
- Mindfulness and emotional regulation

## Minimum viable system

### Sleep

- Target: stable sleep/wake window.
- Track: sleep hours, quality, wake energy.

### Training

- Track sessions per week.
- Focus on strength, mobility, conditioning.

### Food

- Keep meals simple and repeatable.
- Avoid overcomplicated planning.

### Smoking

- Track cigarettes honestly.
- Prefer reducing toward full cessation, not symbolic reduction.

### Stress

- 5-minute decompression after work.
- Weekly reflection.
