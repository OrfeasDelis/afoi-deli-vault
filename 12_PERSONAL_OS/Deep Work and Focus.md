# Deep Work and Focus

## Purpose

Protect your best thinking from daily operational noise.

## Deep work targets

- Business system design
- Supplier strategy
- Website/content direction
- Scavolini expansion
- Hotel/real estate decisions
- Personal learning

## Rules

- One strategic block per week minimum.
- Write decisions, do not only think them.
- Every repeated frustration becomes a system note.
- Every system note can become automation.

## Focus prompt

> What is the bottleneck that, if clarified, makes ten future actions easier?
