# Leadership Standards

## Operating standards

- Calm under pressure
- Precise communication
- No vague promises
- Own the bottleneck
- Document the rule
- Respect people, but do not tolerate repeated chaos
- Build systems, not heroic dependence

## Team principle

If a person repeats a mistake, create:

1. a clearer instruction
2. a checklist
3. a data field
4. a review mechanism
5. then accountability
