# Mindfulness and Reflection

## Purpose

Not softness.  
Strategic self-command.

## Reflection prompts

- What am I avoiding?
- What am I performing instead of solving?
- Where am I chasing intensity instead of clarity?
- What would a calmer, stronger version of me do?
- What is the mask I am wearing today?
- What truth would simplify this?

## Weekly reflection

Write one page per week:

- What happened
- What I felt
- What I learned
- What I will change
