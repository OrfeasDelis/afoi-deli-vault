# Stock House E-shop Concept

## Concept

A curated digital layer for available/stocked items, outlet opportunities, samples, or ready-to-deliver materials.

## Warning

Do not build this before data is clean.

## Required foundation

- Product database
- Stock status
- Photos
- Prices
- Packaging
- Delivery rules
- Internal approval
- Website CMS/product structure

## Possible positioning

Not a cheap marketplace.  
A curated “available now” material library with Afoi Deli taste and reliability.
