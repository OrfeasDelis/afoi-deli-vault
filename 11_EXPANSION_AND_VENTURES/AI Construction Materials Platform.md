# AI Construction Materials Platform

## Concept

A future platform that uses supplier/product/order intelligence to help architects, contractors, and distributors choose, price, compare, and coordinate materials.

## Why you have an edge

- You live the operational pain.
- You know suppliers.
- You know Greek market reality.
- You know architects/contractors/clients.
- You have real order/status/logistics workflows.
- You can test inside Afoi Deli before externalizing.

## Possible modules

- Product/specification search
- Supplier comparison
- Lead time intelligence
- Alternative recommendation
- Packaging calculator
- Quote builder
- Order status control
- Architect material boards
- Distributor community intelligence
- Project material OS

## MVP path

1. Build internal Afoi Deli OS.
2. Turn repeated logic into tools.
3. Use website content to attract users.
4. Offer architect-facing material intelligence.
5. Consider B2B platform only after internal proof.
