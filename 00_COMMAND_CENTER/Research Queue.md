# Research Queue

> [!tip] The no-friction capture surface for supplier research
> Drop a supplier, a collection, or a URL here the moment it comes up — no bot, no app, no structure required. This is the **manual, always-works** version of the [[Hermes Telegram Capture Queue]] (still unbuilt). When there's time, drain the queue by running the [[Supplier Research Workflow]] on each row and ticking it done.

## How to use
- **Add:** append a line under *Pending* — `supplier / collection / url + one-word why`. That's it.
- **Drain:** pick a row, run the [[Supplier Research Workflow]] ("research &lt;supplier&gt;"), save the dossier, move the row to *Done*.
- A row is **done** when it has produced a findable supplier note — not when everything's been said.
