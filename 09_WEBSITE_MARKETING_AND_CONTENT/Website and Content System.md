# Website and Content System

## Website role

The website should evolve from a static presence into a living material intelligence platform.

## Main sections

- Products
- Brands
- Scavolini Kitchens
- Projects / Case Studies
- Moodboard / Journal
- Company
- Contact / Showrooms
- Architect resources
- Future: Stock House / selected available items
- Future: client/project portal

## Content pillars

1. Material education
2. Brand storytelling
3. Project case studies
4. Technical guides
5. Moodboards and applications
6. Kitchen inspiration
7. Outdoor / pool / villa guidance
8. Greek market problem-solving

## Future website stack direction

From past strategic thinking:

- Next.js style frontend
- Headless CMS or structured content layer
- Product/brand database
- SEO pages
- Editorial journal
- Later: connection to internal product/order database

## Do not build first

Do not rush into a full e-shop before product data is clean.

## Build first

- Brand pages
- Product category pages
- Application guides
- Case studies
- Inquiry forms
- Collection request flows
- Architect sample request flows
