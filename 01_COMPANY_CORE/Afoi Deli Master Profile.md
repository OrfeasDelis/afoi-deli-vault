# Afoi Deli Floor + Bath — Master Profile

## One-line identity

**Afoi Deli Floor + Bath** is an Athens-based family business and premium distributor of architectural materials: tiles, surfaces, bathrooms, sanitary ware, faucets, furniture, accessories, wellness products, and Scavolini kitchens.

## Core positioning

> Architecture. Surfaces. Quiet precision.

*(Canonical home of this line: [[Brand DNA]] §Signature phrases — OV-15, 2026-07-19.)*

The company sits between:

- Italian / European manufacturers
- Greek architects and designers
- contractors and developers
- homeowners and villa/hotel owners
- showroom-led retail clients
- project-based specification and supply
