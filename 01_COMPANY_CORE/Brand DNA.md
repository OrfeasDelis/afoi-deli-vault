# Brand DNA

> [!note] Positioning-line ownership (OV-15, ruled 2026-07-19)
> This note is the **canonical home** of the positioning line *"Architecture. Surfaces. Quiet precision."* — [[Afoi Deli Master Profile]] §Core positioning quotes it. Per the stratum ruling (baseline §17-1, split), the voice layer here awaits Orfeas's re-authoring or explicit claim.

## Essence

Afoi Deli is not loud luxury.  
It is **quiet precision**: materials, surfaces, architecture, trust, and taste.

## Tone

- Premium but human
- Calm, exact, useful
- Architectural
- Detail-driven
- Warm without being casual
- Greek market-aware
- Italian material culture-aware

## Avoid

- Generic luxury clichés
- Overpromising
- “AI-ish” language
- Fake minimalism with no operational depth
- Catalogue dumping without taste
- Becoming only an e-shop

## Signature phrases

- Architecture. Surfaces. Quiet precision.
- Materials chosen with care, delivered with responsibility.
- From sample to site.
- European materials, local execution.
- Design support with operational discipline.

## Brand pillars

1. **Material expertise**
2. **Architectural taste**
3. **Supplier access**
4. **Operational reliability**
5. **Project memory**
6. **Human service**
7. **Digital transformation**

## Future persona

Afoi Deli can become:

- a premium material distributor
- a design/specification partner
- a digital material library
- a project supply control tower
- a content authority in Greek architecture/materials
- a bridge between Italian manufacturers and Greek projects
