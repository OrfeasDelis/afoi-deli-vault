# Conduit - Plus Interiors

> [!quote]
> A **conduit**, not a counterparty. The brand is what we care about; the office is the channel it currently arrives through. Keep a working relationship warm in practice and clear-eyed on paper. See [[Afoi Deli — Operating Doctrine#The responsibility doctrine]].

## What Plus Interiors is to us

The independent representative office through which several of our heaviest Italian houses reach the Greek market — **[[Supplier - Kronos|Kronos]]**, **[[Supplier - Cielo|Cielo]]** and **[[Supplier - Fantini|Fantini]]** among them. It is run as a family office, and by now a multi-generational one — which makes it a structural mirror of Afoi Deli itself.

## The people

- **Kostas Tzanidis** — principal; the relationship's centre of gravity. Genuine material expertise, real guidance, always willing to help. (The "heavy brand, forgiven for being a little late" character that fits his houses fits him, too.)
- **Alexandra Kalogeropoulou** — his wife; part of the office.
- **George Tzanidis** — their son, now in the business. Roughly Orfeas's age: a **generational mirror** — succession happening on both sides of the table at once.

## The founding act (~10–15 years ago)

Your father, [[The Heart|Kostas]], gave Tzanidis the hint to chase representation of **[[Supplier - Cielo|Cielo]]** — then little-known sanitary ware, now a flagship that majorly raised the office's level. He **created the circumstances**; loyalty followed as a *consequence* of generosity that was also shrewd. The maxim in the wild — [[The Heart|"create the circumstances so others ask — never ask."]] It is why the Cielo line, and the Plus Interiors relationship, sit close to the heart of the business.

## The power geometry — never forget

> [!warning] The conduit is optional; the brand is not
> The office takes a **% of sales** as income, but the brands could in principle be sold direct. The representative's position is therefore **structurally replaceable** — *in most cases they need us more than we need them.* Friends up to a point; commercial past it, and if a line is crossed it gets a quiet reality check.
>
> **What we care about is the brand, not the office.** A relationship that works does not need to be meddled with — stay warm in practice — but file the truth: when problems arise, the client turns to **Afoi Deli** first, not to Tzanidis and not even to the manufacturer. (See [[Afoi Deli — Operating Doctrine#The responsibility doctrine]].)
