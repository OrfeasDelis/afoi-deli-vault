# Scavolini — Collections Reference

> [!info] What this is
> Model-level detail for [[Supplier - Scavolini]] — kitchens, bathrooms, living, and the **countertop materials** that drive our cross-sell. Summary depth; exact finishes, modules and prices live in the per-model catalogues and in real quote data. Source: scavolini.com (ingested 2026-06-16).

> [!warning] List completeness & link caveats
> scavolini.com is a JavaScript single-page app (WebFetch blocked, lazy-loaded), so the model roster is **strong but not provably exhaustive** — a few current models may be missing and a few here may be retired. Designers marked *(to verify)* are best-guess (mostly in-house **Vuesse**) and not confirmed on the official model page. Catalogue PDFs are served client-side per model ("Download the catalogue" on each model page, thron.com CDN) — only Foodshelf + LiberaMente were captured directly.

## Kitchens — Classic (country / English / shabby-chic)

- **Exclusiva** *(Vuesse)* — top-of-range luxury: leather doors, gold/chrome capitals, Swarovski handles, bevelled-mirror hood; marble (Emperador, Nero Marquinia) / granite (Golden Leaf, Steel Grey) worktops, 6 cm extra thickness.
- **Baltimora** *(Vuesse)* — framed-door classic/country; shaped lines, traditional handles; warm, romantic.
- **Favilla** *(Vuesse)* — shabby-chic English-style framed door; vintage, romantic.
- **Family** *(Vuesse)* — shabby-chic family kitchen; accessible, adaptable (sold online).
- **Colony** *(Vuesse)* — country/English style with slat detailing; family-friendly.

## Kitchens — Contemporary (minimal / modular)

- **LiberaMente** *(Vuesse)* — **best-seller**; minimalist flat-door system, open modules, very wide colour range; supports sink-in-top (e.g. Calce Nero porcelain stoneware). Catalogue: https://www.scavolini.com/ww/living/living-liberamente/downloads/3820/203/131_19_LiberaMente2020_9.pdf
- **Foodshelf** *(Ora-ïto)* — open kitchen-living concept; horizontal woodlook shelves across kitchen + living; 22 mm angled-grip flat door. Catalogue: https://scavolini-cdn.thron.com/delivery/public/document/scavolini/768a8564-6b65-4668-92a5-4a8c0b4c7aa9/nuctwf/WEB/Foodshelf.pdf
- **Carattere** *(Vuesse)* — contemporary framed door, English register; chic, elegant; quartz worktops (e.g. Amazon).
- **DeLinea** *(Vuesse)* — flat door in steel/brass/bronze finishes; elegant, glamour; shown with Kelya Dekton.
- **Essenza** *(Vuesse)* — flat-door; elegant, natural, customisable, open-space.
- **Musa** *(Vuesse)* — essential, clean, minimal.
- **Lumina** · **Libra** · **Mira** *(Vuesse)* — recent contemporary modular models.
- **Dandy Plus** *(Vuesse)* — compact flat-door; smart, technological, young.
- **Mood** · **Motus** · **Qi** *(Vuesse — to verify)* — contemporary modular (Motus & Qi also offered as Living / bathroom ranges).
- **MIA by Carlo Cracco** *(Vuesse × chef Carlo Cracco)* — professional-inspired home kitchen.

## Kitchens — Design (modern / industrial / fashion)

- **Diesel Social Kitchen** · **Diesel Open Workshop** · **Diesel Get Together** *(Diesel Living with Scavolini)* — industrial/loft; flat door, glass, steel; fashion, colourful, open-space.
- **Boxi** *(Studio Rainlight — Yorgo Lykouria)* — minimal customisable flat-door system; sociable, young.
- **Formalia** *(Vittore Niolu)* — spans kitchen, living & bathroom; elegant, sophisticated (Coral Clay quartz worktop).
- **Sax** *(Karim Rashid — to verify)* — decorative melamine framed doors; tactile.
- **Tetrix** *(Michael Young — to verify)* · **Flux / Flux Swing** *(Giugiaro Design — to verify)* — design models (attribution to confirm; Flux Swing won a Good Design Award 2016).

## Kitchens — Outdoor

- **Formalia Outdoor** *(Vittore Niolu)* — Scavolini's first outdoor kitchen; weather-suited materials, modular.

## Bathrooms

- **Idro** *(Studio Castiglia Associati)* — horizontal grooves to open doors; wide glossy/matt lacquer range.
- **Diesel Misfits** *(Diesel Living with Scavolini)* — industrial-twist bathroom furniture.
- **Rivo** — suspended modules, 45° grooves, open shelves. · **Aquo** — extensive finishes for personalisation. · **Lagu** — ergonomic door design (also Living). · **Font** · **Juno** · **Lido** · **Magnifica** *(Vuesse — to verify)* — bathroom furniture ranges.

## Living & wardrobes

- **BoxLife** *(Studio Rainlight)* — multifunctional living modules: bookcases, TV stations, shelves, drawers.
- **LiberaMente Living** *(Vuesse)* / **Foodshelf Living** *(Ora-ïto)* — living counterparts of the kitchen systems.
- **Walk-in Fluida** *(Vuesse)* — walk-in wardrobe / closet system.

## Countertops / worktops (the cross-sell)

Official Scavolini worktop materials — map onto our suppliers (see [[Supplier - Scavolini#Countertops — the cross-sell pathway]]):

- **Porcelain stoneware / gres** — heat-resistant, near-seamless, sink-integrable (e.g. Calce Nero). Thin **Kerlite** laminated stoneware too. → [[Supplier - Kronos]] / [[Large Slabs Knowledge]].
- **Natural stone** — marble (Nero Marquinia, Emperador), granite (Golden Leaf, Steel Grey); up to 6 cm extra thickness on premium models. → stone suppliers *(to be created)*.
- **Quartz** (engineered; Coral Clay, Amazon) · **Dekton** (Cosentino sintered ultracompact; e.g. Kelya) · **laminate** (versatile, not heat-resistant; e.g. Mune Grey) · **steel** (hygienic, professional look).
