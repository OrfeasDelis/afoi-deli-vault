# Supplier - Scavolini

> [!quote]
> The **kitchen** identity brand — and the keystone of our expansion from surfaces into whole-interior authority. Full strategy: [[Scavolini Kitchen Expansion]]. See [[Afoi Deli — Operating Doctrine#The core asset the uplift engine]].

## What Scavolini is to us

The natural next layer of the trade. After tiling and wood parquet, **kitchens are the must** — and Scavolini, *the* renowned Italian kitchen brand, was an ambitious but logical expansion. The rationale (Orfeas):

- **One supplier, one channel, one trusted partner** — clients group the whole house under us; a single line of communication for orders and the project. The convenience is itself the offer.
- **Countertop pathways** — worktops in 6/12 mm porcelain slabs or natural stone pull our own [[#Countertops — the cross-sell pathway|slab & stone suppliers]] into each kitchen (new sales, more of the project through us).
- **Cashflow / wallet share** — each kitchen "drains" a larger slice of the client's total project spend, not just the surfaces.
- **Status & branding** — partnering a renowned Italian house is uplifting for the company and showcases our full range of expertise.

## Distinctive service / strategic angle

- **The Scavolini Store model** — a single-brand retail format (unique in Italy at launch) with the **service bundled into the price**: on-site measuring, computerised design, quotation, **transport + installation included**. This is the template for the whole-interior, one-partner experience we're building.
- **Whole-home under one brand** — kitchen + living + bathroom from the same house, so a client can furnish the core of the home in one language.
- **Value positioning** — repeatedly awarded the German Quality Institute's *Top Qualità-Prezzo* (best quality/price), incl. "Top Qualità-Prezzo 2024" (4th consecutive year). A premium-brand halo at an accessible tier (a Basic/Easy line exists alongside premium and designer lines).

## Company, ownership & sustainability

- **Founded:** **1961**, Pesaro, by brothers **Valter & Elvino Scavolini** (the current S.p.A. carries a 1967 registry incorporation — treat 1961 as brand origin, 1967 as the legal shell). 60+ years, family-run into the third generation (a "patto di famiglia" governs succession — a mirror of our own).
- **Scale:** Italy's largest kitchen group — ~€220M revenue, ~650–700 staff, a ~240,000 m² automated, fully made-in-Italy complex at Montelabbate; 1,300+ showrooms across five continents.
- **Sustainability — a real selling point** (the "**Scavolini Green Mind**" programme): ISO 9001 / 14001 / 45001; **Made in Italy** (COSMOB 2018); **CQP Product Circularity ~96%** (2021); **FSC** Chain-of-Custody (2021) + Product (2022, C168055); **Indoor Hi-Quality GOLD** (low-VOC, LEED-compliant A++); Ecological Panel (100% recycled wood); renewable electricity since 2008. Strong credentials for spec/contract work.

## The channel — how Scavolini reaches us

Scavolini sells through **mono-brand Scavolini Stores + a dealer network** — *not* an independent rep office like our tile/bath conduits, so there is **no `Conduit -` note** for it. The publicly-listed Athens Scavolini Store is **OMETRY** — a **separate business**; do not conflate it with our channel. Our specific dealer arrangement and terms are captured in the `[!question]` under *Commercial profile*.

## Countertops — the cross-sell pathway

The strategic hook: every Scavolini kitchen needs a worktop, and worktops route through **our** suppliers. Scavolini's official worktop materials:

- **Porcelain stoneware / gres** (heat-resistant, near-seamless, sink-integrated) and thin **Kerlite** → our porcelain slabs: [[Supplier - Kronos]] (large slabs / K-LAB) · [[Large Slabs Knowledge]].
- **Natural stone** — marble (Nero Marquinia, Emperador), granite (Golden Leaf, Steel Grey), up to 6 cm extra thickness on premium models → respective stone suppliers *(notes to be created)*.
- **Quartz** (e.g. Coral Clay, Amazon), **Dekton** (Cosentino sintered), **laminate**, **steel** — Scavolini's own / third-party.
