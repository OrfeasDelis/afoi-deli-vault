## Full-body porcelain — floor lines (stone / concrete / volcanic looks)

### Déchirer — *unglazed full-body porcelain, concrete look* · Patricia Urquiola
Concrete-opacity surface with subtle "torn memory" bas-relief. Lines: Neutral (Ecru), Decor (Nero), Esagona/hexagon (Piombo), Trace (Cemento). 60×60, 60×120, 120×120 + hexagon; 12 mm. Large-format extension: **Déchirer XL**.
Page: https://www.mutina.it/en/collection/dechirer-by-patricia-urquiola

### Pico — *unglazed full-body porcelain, dot-relief* · Ronan & Erwan Bouroullec
Regular-irregular dot texture in two surfaces — Down (sunken) and Up (raised). Blanc, Gris, Anthracite, Argile + Pico Satin + Red/Blue dot variants. 60×60 / 60×120 / 120×120; 12 mm; indoor/outdoor.
Catalogue: https://www.mutina.it/downloads/2534/155/Pico_catalogue_2021.pdf · Technical: https://www.mutina.it/downloads/2534/158/TechnicalFeatures_Pico.zip

### Kosei — *extruded unglazed porcelain, volcanic look* · Vincent Van Duysen
Inspired by obsidian/volcanic glass; matt, homogeneous, hard. Bone, Storm, Terra, Grey Green, Dark Grey. 60×60, 15×90/60/30/15 + chevron; 10 mm.
Catalogue: https://www.mutina.it/downloads/10930/886/KOSEI.pdf

### Time — *unglazed porcelain, weathered stone look* · Barber & Osgerby
Natural-weathering concept; Rough and Smooth finishes. 8 geological colours (Etna Black, Dakota Grey, Dover White, Salta Red, Highland Green, Virginia Blue, Sahara Sand, Atlas Brown). 20.5×20.5 / 3.9×20.5 / 3.9×3.9; 12 mm.
Catalogue: https://www.mutina.it/downloads/12631/1055/Catalogo_TIME_A.pdf · Technical: https://www.mutina.it/downloads/12631/1057/TechnicalFeatures_TIME.zip

## Glazed designer tiles (wall / floor)

### Azulej — *rectified glazed porcelain (digital print)* · Patricia Urquiola
Revives handcrafted majolica / hydraulic-cement tiles in unsaturated neutrals. Patterns Cubo, Trevo, Estrela, Flores, Renda, Combination, Trama — patchwork. 20×20; 10 mm. Wallpaper Award 2013, Interior Innovation Award 2014.

### Mater — *glazed & unglazed rectified porcelain* · Patricia Urquiola
Glaze-as-protagonist; industrial body, artisanal feel. Uni (Beige/Moka) + Segno glossy decors. 15×60, 60×60, 120×120; 9 mm.
Catalogue: https://www.mutina.it/downloads/11989/1091/MATER_PS.pdf · Technical: https://www.mutina.it/downloads/11989/1025/TechnicalFeatures_Mater.zip

### Mews — *mass-coloured glazed porcelain* · Barber & Osgerby
London-mews inspiration; 9 shades (Chalk, Fog, Pigeon, Lead, Ink, Soot, Green, Light Blue, Pink). 5.5×45, 11×11 + chevrons; 9 mm.
Catalogue: https://www.mutina.it/downloads/2473/1093/MEWS_PS.pdf · Technical: https://www.mutina.it/downloads/2473/1102/TechnicalFeatures_Mews.zip

### Tex — *glazed porcelain, knit-textile look* · Raw Edges
Knitted-stitch texture; irregular saturated glaze. 8 colours × 3 patterns. 11.5×20 + 35×40.4 runner on mesh; 10 mm.
Catalogue: https://www.mutina.it/downloads/2626/192/TEX_catalogo.pdf · Technical: https://www.mutina.it/downloads/2626/344/TechnicalFeatures_Tex.zip

### Aurore — *glazed non-rectified porcelain* · Vincent Van Duysen
Soft chalk-drawing aesthetic; Light Blue/Green/Grey, Charcoal; Solid + Grid decors. 20.5×20.5; 9.5 mm.
Catalogue: https://www.mutina.it/downloads/15061/1531/Mutina_AURORE_LR.pdf · Technical: https://www.mutina.it/downloads/15061/1533/TechnicalFeatures_Aurore.zip

### DIN — *glazed pressed ceramic, modular grid* · Konstantin Grcic
Modular grid mosaic (15×15, 7.4×15, 7.4×7.4, 3.6×7.4) + special profiles. 8 colours, matt & glossy. 9 mm.
Catalogue: https://www.mutina.it/downloads/8275/540/Catalogue_DIN_2022.pdf · Technical: https://www.mutina.it/downloads/8275/544/TechnicalFeatures_DIN.zip

Three-dimensional bas-relief, chiaroscuro light play. Decors Code, Patchwork, Garland, Cloud; white/powder/black. 9 mm.

Nature-inspired 3D modules (Honeycomb, Rock, Air, Wind, Rain). Bianco/Grigio/Fango/Nero + glossy metallics. 25–30 cm modules; 5–8 mm.

## Terracotta bricks & wood

### Brac — *extruded terracotta, perforated 3D brick (Bricks)* · Nathalie Du Pasquier
3D perforated terracotta bricks for light/shadow screens. Argilla (natural) + Bianco, Salvia, Marrone, Nero (glazed). 12.5×22×10 cm. Interior Design Best of Year 2020.
Catalogue: https://www.mutina.it/downloads/5823/308/Brac_October_2020.pdf · Technical: https://www.mutina.it/downloads/5823/320/TechnicalFeatures_Brac.zip

### Tierras — *porcelain + extruded terracotta, earth/clay look* · Patricia Urquiola
The "Mediterranean sedimentation" range. **Industrial:** porcelain on a recycled-ceramic base (incl. Frame, Triomix), up to 120×120. **Artisanal:** extruded-terracotta Roman bricks (Little/Big Roman, Bis-cotto, Little L, Base).
Page: https://www.mutina.it/en/tierras/

### Pico Bois — *European oak flooring (Wood/Accents)* · Ronan & Erwan Bouroullec
Mutina's first wooden floor — oak slats carrying the Pico dot pattern; pairs with porcelain Pico. 7×49 cm; 14 mm.
