## Washbasin collections (the core range)

### I Catini — *bowl / catino washbasins*
Modern reinterpretation of early-1900s bowl basins — Round, Oval, Rectangular, Double; countertop / semi-recessed / wall-hung; hand-decorated colour. **Red Dot** winner.
Catalogue: https://img.ceramicacielo.it/download_cataloghi/ICatini22.pdf

### Le Giare — *sculptural minimalist basins*
Architectural jar/"giara" form by **Claudio Silvestrin**. Good Design + German Design Award + ADI Design Index.

### Marcel — *freestanding basin + cabinet*
Design series by **Andrea Parisio & Giuseppe Pezzano**.
Catalogue: https://img.ceramicacielo.it/download_cataloghi/catalogo_Marcel.pdf

### Plinio / Delfo / Multiplo — *pedestal & modular basins*
Column-inspired (Plinio; Good Design + Red Dot), Delfo, and modular Multiplo — by Parisio & Pezzano; shared catalogue.
Catalogue: https://img.ceramicacielo.it/download_cataloghi/Plinio-delfo-multiplo.pdf

### Enjoy / Amedeo — *minimalist basins (Karim Rashid)*
Enjoy: wall-mounted, Good Design 2016. Amedeo: Red Dot + Good Design.

### Tiberino (Tiberio) — *cylindrical freestanding basin*
Freestanding column basin; German Design Award (2016).

### Theo (Theo 78 / Theo 175) — *De Stijl geometric basins*
Pure essential lines inspired by De Stijl; Theo 175 a 2023 "Badge Winner".
Catalogue: https://img.ceramicacielo.it/download_cataloghi/Theo23.pdf

### Itaca — *organic / "primordial" basins*
Organic primordial aesthetic (Luca Cimarra / Parisio-Pezzano); ADA23 Design Award.

### Kanto / Otis — *contemporary & pedestal basins*
Both by Parisio & Pezzano (Otis a redesigned pedestal basin).

### Milano — *minimalist Milanese basins/bidets*
Urban-sophistication line for modern interiors.

### Les Petites — *compact basins*
Small-format countertop & wall-mounted washbasins.
Catalogue: https://img.ceramicacielo.it/download_cataloghi/LesPetites23.pdf

### Filo — *modular basin + wood vanity*
Minimalist modular washbasin with wooden vanity units.

### Siwa · Sella · Kyros · Elle (Ovale) · Le Bacinelle · Sessanta — *further basin lines*
Kyros (Good Design), Elle Ovale (Archiproducts Design Award), Le Bacinelle (bowl basins), Sella (saddle form), Siwa, Sessanta (design-focused).

Freestanding bathtub with coordinated vanities.

## Colour & finish programmes (across collections)

- **Le Terre di Cielo** & **Le Acque di Cielo** — exclusive nature-derived palettes (~22 nuances together). Cielo's signature.
- **Extrà** — proprietary ultra-thin (~5 mm) coloured ceramic.
- **Finishes systems:** FENIX multistrato · wood species · metals & leather · Essential Colors.
- **WC systems:** RIMLESS 2.0.1 (rimless flush) · RESEAT.
