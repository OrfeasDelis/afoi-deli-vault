## What Cielo is to us

A premium Italian **sanitary-ware** house — washbasins, WCs, bathtubs, ceramic tops, furniture — built on hand craftsmanship and, above all, **colour**. Where most ceramic sanitaryware is white and industrial, Cielo's identity is the coloured, hand-finished, art-directed bathroom. That matches the showroom's register exactly, and it is the brand whose pursuit, years ago, raised the level of the whole [[Conduit - Plus Interiors|Plus Interiors]] relationship — the founding act of [[The Heart|"create the circumstances so others ask"]].

## Distinctive service / strategic angle

The reasons Cielo is an identity brand, not a commodity supplier:

1. **Colour as signature** — exclusive palettes **Le Terre di Cielo** and **Le Acque di Cielo** (~22 nature-derived nuances together). Cielo is the colour pioneer of Italian bathroom ceramics.
2. **Extrà** — proprietary ultra-thin (~5 mm) coloured ceramic; a technical signature few sanitaryware houses can match.
3. **"Cultura delle mani"** — hand-finishing that allows near-bespoke personalisation.
4. **Design-award validation** — Red Dot, German Design Award, Good Design Award, ADI Design Index across many collections; a deep external-designer roster (Andrea Parisio & Giuseppe Pezzano, Karim Rashid, Claudio Silvestrin, 5.5 designstudio, Marco Piva, Matteo Thun, Paolo D'Arrigo).

## Company, ownership & sustainability

- **Founded:** operating company dated **1997** (`likely` — LinkedIn/Kompass); brand created by **Alessio Coramusi**, descendant of a Civita Castellana family of master ceramicists tracing to the early 1900s (artisanal lineage → modern brand).
- **Ownership:** 100% owned by **IBD – Italian Bathroom Design**, whose majority shareholder is the **listed Mittel Group** (Milan exchange). IBD consolidates several Civita Castellana sanitaryware names (also linked to Galassia, Disegno Ceramica, Catalano). `confidence: verified` (own site).
