# Supplier - Fantini

> [!quote]
> An **identity brand** — the design reference point in faucets, and the *only* tap maker in the Altagamma Foundation. The fitting that finishes a Cielo basin or a Kronos surface. See [[Afoi Deli — Operating Doctrine#The core asset the uplift engine]].

## What Fantini is to us

The premium **taps & fittings** house in our range — the jewellery of the bathroom. Where the tile and the basin set the scene, the faucet is the touchpoint, and Fantini is the author-driven, design-museum-grade option (Compasso d'Oro, MoMA). It completes a specified bathroom at the level of the surfaces around it, and — like Kronos and Cielo — reaches us through [[Conduit - Plus Interiors|Plus Interiors]].

## Product range & collections

> [!info] Source: fantini.it, ingested 2026-06-16. Premium taps & fittings; the full per-series list, finishes and catalogue + technical PDF links live in [[Fantini — Collections Reference]].

**Types:** washbasin mixers (deck/wall), bathtub mixers (floor/wall), shower systems & showerheads, kitchen mixers, accessories, and design-led **marble/stone washbasins** (Fontane Bianche). **Materials:** lead-free brass, stainless steel, marble/stone. **The finish programme is the signature** — a broad PVD range (Chrome, Matt Black/White, Polished Nickel, Gun Metal, British Gold, Copper, Deep Black, Mokka, Brushed Steel Look, Pure Brass, Raw Metal PVD, Brushed Stainless) plus marble handle options and RAL on request (I Balocchi).

## Distinctive service / strategic angle

1. **Author-driven design as moat** — the **only faucet maker in Fondazione Altagamma**; commissions named architects/designers; products in **MoMA** (Calibro, 1981); **Compasso d'Oro ADI 2020** (AK/25, Paik Sun Kim).
2. **ABOUTWATER** — the co-brand with **Boffi** (since 2011): a top-tier designer line (AF/21, AL/23, AK/25, AA/27 + the Ex.Series PIPE/ECLIPSE/GARDEN) that places Fantini inside Boffi's high-end bathroom-system distribution.
3. **Deep PVD & material programme** — ~13 PVD finishes, marble/stone handles; lead-free brass / eco stainless.
4. **Experiential brand** — **Casa Fantini-Lake Time** hotel (architect Piero Lissoni, 2017) as a living showcase; the social "**100 Fontane: Fantini for Africa**" water programme (Burundi), partly funded by I Balocchi sales.

## Company, ownership & sustainability

- **Founded:** **1947** by brothers **Giovanni & Ersilio Fantini** in Pella; design "in the company's DNA" from the start (milestone: **I Balocchi**, 1976 — the first coloured taps). CEO **Daniela Fantini**.
- **Ownership:** independent, **family-owned** — itself the controlling entity, no parent group. (Distinct from the ABOUTWATER co-brand it runs *with* Boffi.)
- **Sustainability:** lead-free brass / eco stainless meeting international health standards (ABOUTWATER line); landscape-preservation messaging around Lake Orta; the "100 Fontane" programme. ⚠️ Specific certifications (ISO 9001/14001, water-efficiency) were **not** confirmed (the company/sustainability page errored at fetch) — `needs_check`.
