## ABOUTWATER — the Boffi · Fantini designer line

### AF/21 — *minimalist; discs, cylinders & marble handle* · Naoto Fukasawa
Forms reduced to pure icon; marble handle (Carrara White / Marquinia Black) like a river-smoothed stone. Finishes: Chrome, Brushed Stainless, Matt Gun Metal/British Gold/Copper PVD.

### AL/23 — *minimalist; fountains & industrial engines* · Piero Lissoni
Vocation for simplicity; practical, tactile, precise. Washbasin mixer + shower system. Finishes: Polished Chrome, Matt White, Matt Black.

Oriental painting meets contemporary minimalism — bold lines, white space, black accents. **Compasso d'Oro ADI 2020.** Finish incl. Matt Gun Metal PVD.

## Marble / stone & iconic

### Fontane Bianche — *marble/stone washbasins + Gun Metal taps* · Elisa Ossino
Circle-meets-square geometry; square marble washbasin with hemispherical bowl; named for the white-sand springs near Syracuse, Sicily. Taps in Matt Gun Metal PVD.
Catalogue: https://res.cloudinary.com/thinking/image/upload/fantiniProduzione/CATALOGO-FONTANEBIANCHE_ENG_1617953721.pdf · Technical: https://res.cloudinary.com/thinking/image/upload/fantiniProduzione/MANUALE_TECNICO-FONTANEBIANCHE_ENG_1775570932.pdf

### I Balocchi — *iconic coloured "Pop" faucet* · Davide Mercatali & Paolo Pedrizzetti
The first coloured taps (1976; reissued 2012) — rounded Pop shapes. Finishes: Red-Chrome (iconic), Matt Black, Matt White; **RAL on request**. Sales fund "100 Fontane: Fantini for Africa".
Catalogue: https://res.cloudinary.com/thinking/image/upload/fantiniProduzione/CATALOGO-IBALOCCHI_ENG_1770040666.pdf · Technical: https://res.cloudinary.com/thinking/image/upload/fantiniProduzione/MANUALE_TECNICO-IBALOCCHI_ENG_1775657621.pdf
