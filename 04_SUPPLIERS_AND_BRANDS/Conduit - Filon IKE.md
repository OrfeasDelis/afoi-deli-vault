# Conduit - Filon IKE

> [!quote]
> A **conduit**, not a counterparty — the same doctrine as [[Conduit - Plus Interiors]]. The brand is what we care about; the office is the channel. See [[Afoi Deli — Operating Doctrine#The responsibility doctrine]].

## What Filon IKE is to us

The representative office through which **[[Supplier - Mutina|Mutina]]** — and its other subbrands — reach the Greek market. Led by **Alexandros Simos**.

## Character

> [!info] From Orfeas (relationship knowledge)
> A **very professional and punctual** office overall, across all its subbrands — a deliberate contrast to the "heavy brand, forgiven for being a little late" register of [[Conduit - Plus Interiors]]. Punctuality and professionalism are the relationship's signature; it makes Filon an easy, reliable channel to work through.

## The power geometry

> [!warning] The conduit is optional; the brand is not
> As with every representative office: Filon takes a **% of sales**, and the brands could in principle be sold direct, so the position is **structurally replaceable**. Here the relationship is easy and reliable — which is exactly why it is kept warm in practice — but the doctrine still holds: when problems arise, the client turns to **Afoi Deli** first, not to the office and not to the manufacturer.
