# Supplier Master Index

## Purpose

One canonical map for all suppliers.

## Supplier note naming

Use:

```text
Supplier - Supplier Name
```

Each supplier lives in its own folder: `Suppliers/<Name>/Supplier - <Name>.md`. Short `[[Supplier - <Name>]]` wikilinks resolve by filename regardless of folder — prefer them over full paths.

## Supplier intelligence checklist

For every supplier capture:

- Category
- Brands
- Contact persons
- Agency
- Price lists
- Catalogues
- Discounts
- Payment terms
- Lead times
- Packaging rules
- Loading habits
- Common mistakes
- Best products
- High-margin products
- Difficult products
- Email style
- Relationship quality
- Strategic role

## Strategic categories

| Category | Meaning |
|---|---|

| Growth | Important for future strategy |

| Experimental | Test relationship |
