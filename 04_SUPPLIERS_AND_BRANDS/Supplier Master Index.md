## Supplier intelligence checklist

For every supplier capture:

- Category
- Brands
- Contact persons
- Agency
- Price lists
- Catalogues
- Discounts
- Payment terms
- Lead times
- Packaging rules
- Loading habits
- Common mistakes
- Best products
- High-margin products
- Difficult products
- Email style
- Relationship quality
- Strategic role

## Strategic categories

| Category | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Core | Essential supplier, frequent orders |
| Growth | Important for future strategy |
| Prestige | Brand halo / showroom value |
| Utility | Practical supplier, operationally useful |
| Experimental | Test relationship |
| Problematic | High friction, needs controls |
